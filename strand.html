Stand Alone Senior High School Academic Strands

Discover our programs, events, and activities that make our school a wonderful place for students to learn and grow.

Academic Strand


Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics, prepares senior high school students for careers in related fields. It includes specialized subjects like Physics, Biology, Chemistry, and Calculus, emphasizing critical thinking, problem-solving, and hands-on learning. STEM students often pursue college courses in engineering, medicine, computer science, architecture, and applied sciences.



GAS strand (General Academic Strand) offers a flexible curriculum for senior high school students unsure of their career path. It includes diverse subjects from other strands like STEM, ABM, and HUMSS, providing a well-rounded education. GAS prepares students for various college courses and is ideal for those exploring their interests.



HUMSS strand focuses on Humanities, Social Sciences, and communication skills. It prepares students for careers in fields like Journalism, Education, Law, and Psychology through subjects like Philosophy, Sociology, and Creative Writing. It's ideal for those passionate about social issues and working with people.


Technical /Vocational / Livelihood Strand


ICT strand focuses on technology and digital skills, covering programming, web development, and networking. It prepares students for careers like web developers, IT specialists, and multimedia artists.



EIM strand specializes in electrical installation and maintenance, teaching skills in electrical systems and equipment repair. It prepares students for careers such as electricians, electrical engineers, and maintenance technicians.
